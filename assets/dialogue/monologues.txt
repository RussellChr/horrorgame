# ── Entrance Hall ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[portrait]
Alex
---
A portrait hangs on the wall. The painted eyes seem to follow me wherever
I move. I have heard of optical illusions like this -- clever brushwork
that gives depth to a flat image. But this feels different. This feels
deliberate.
---

[stranger]
Alex
---
There is someone else in this house. That should be reassuring. It is not.
---

# ── Dark Corridor ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[basement_door_locked]
Alex
---
Heavy iron. Cold as winter even in summer. Whoever put this padlock here
wanted very badly for whatever is on the other side to stay there. I need
to find the key before I can go any further.
---

[basement_door_unlocked]
Alex
---
The padlock clicks open. The sound is too loud in the silence. Cold air
breathes up from the darkness below -- stale, still, as though the
basement has not been opened in years. Something down there has been
waiting a long time for someone to open this door.
---

# ── The Library ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[diary]
Alex
---
A leather-bound diary, left open on the desk. The ink is faded but still
legible. I should not read someone else's private thoughts -- but I have a
feeling this is exactly what I am meant to find.
---

# ── Child's Room ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[key]
Alex
---
A small brass key, half-buried in a toy box beneath a threadbare teddy
bear. Why would a child have a key to the basement door? Unless they were
not put there to play -- but to guard it.
---

# ── Entrance Hall items ───────────────────────────────────────────────

[flashlight]
Alex
---
A flashlight. Someone left it here on the floor -- carelessly, or
perhaps deliberately. Either way, I am grateful for it. The darkness
in this house is not natural.
---

# ── Ritual Room ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

[ritual_circle]
Alex
---
The symbols on the floor. I have seen these before -- in the diary.
This is the binding circle. Something was imprisoned here, kept in place
by this pattern carved into the stone. The edges are crumbling. The
binding is breaking.
---
